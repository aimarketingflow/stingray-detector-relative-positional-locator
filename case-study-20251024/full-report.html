5.3 Detection Steps

Step 1: Baseline Scan

Establish normal RF environment:

./baseline-scan-boosted.sh

This creates a reference of normal signals in your area.

Step 2: Quick Check

Check for anomalies:

./quick-check.sh

Compares current signals against baseline. Look for:

  • Very strong signals (-10 to -30 dBm)
  • Unusual frequencies (758-767 MHz, 850-853 MHz)
  • Power changes > 5 dB

Step 3: Directional Scanning (if threat detected)

Launch GUI for directional analysis:

python3 stingray_detector_gui.py

Point antenna in 8 directions to triangulate position.

Step 4: Extended Monitoring

Track over time:

./track-movement.sh 30 60

30 minutes, 1-minute intervals. Confirms if device is stationary or mobile.
